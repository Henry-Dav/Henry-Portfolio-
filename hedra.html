What I noticed

  • Led with the mission before giving the reader a reason to care about it.
  • Four capabilities listed at once, so nothing stood out as the first thing to do.

What changed

  • Named the frustration up front instead of hiding it near the end.
  • Replaced the feature list with one small action and a time estimate.
  • Made support feel like a person answering, not a ticket.
